Detailed Fit Comparison

While the style classic meets current is consistent across our Fit Types, each one is cut
to comfortably fit various bodies and suiting needs. Dive into the main features and
differences to discover which one is made for you because we have something that is!
Jackets and pants don't come in predetermined sets, so you can always choose what
works for you and create your own personalized combination.

We've sketched out our Fit Types to illustrate the specific details of how each is cut. See
for yourself, and read up on the key traits to find the fit for you.

Men's Slim Fit

  • Broad shoulders with a tapered waist for V
    shaped bodies.
  • Narrow waist without much room around the
    midsection.
  • Designed to fit very closely to your body
    with less space between you and the jacket
    compared to Modern
  • Runs one size smaller than our Modern Fit in
    the same size.

   Men's Modern Fit

  • Straighter cut from the shoulders through
    the waist.
  • More room in the waist compared to our Slim
    Fit.
  • Designed with slightly more room between
    your body and the jacket compared to Slim
    Fit.
  • True to size compared to most suiting
    brands, but fits approximately one size larger
    than Slim Fit in the same size.

Women's Fit

  • Classic, menswear-inspired look designed
    or a feminine body.
  • Tapered waistline cinches in to create an
    hourglass shape.
  • Close fit flatters your natural shape without
    feeling too tight or restrictive.
  • Fabric and draping provide comfort and
    allow for a more snug, figure-hugging fit.

Pant Fit Comparison

Men's Slim Fit

  • More closely-fitted through the waist, seat,
    and thigh.
  • Sits lower down on the waist compared to
    Modern Fit.
  • Tapered leg through the calf.
  • Perfect for more narrow legs to get a fitted
    look.

   Men's Modern Fit

  • Roomier through the waist, seat, and thigh,
    without looking baggy or dated.
  • Sits higher on the waist compared to Slim
    Fit.
  • Less of a taper through the calf to allow
    more room.
  • Perfect for athletic or larger legs to get a
    trim, tailored look.

Women's Fit

  • Mid-rise waist.
  • Tapered leg all the way down for a cigarette
    style slim look.
  • Notch at ankle with small side slit for added
    detail and mobility.
  • Perfect for feminine bodies to get a timeless,
    leek look.
